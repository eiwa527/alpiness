About the web

The Typing Game Hub is an interactive web-based application designed to improve users’ typing speed, accuracy, and overall keyboard skills through engaging and enjoyable mini-games. Instead of traditional typing exercises, this platform integrates gameplay elements such as racing, shooting, and timed challenges to make learning more fun and motivating. Users can choose from a variety of typing games, select different difficulty levels, and track their performance through statistics like words per minute (WPM) and accuracy. The system aims to provide an entertaining yet educational experience, making it suitable for students and individuals who want to enhance their typing proficiency in a more dynamic way.
